The Art of Fragrance

At Lume, we believe in the transformative power of scent. Each candle is hand-poured in small batches using the finest natural ingredients, ensuring a clean burn and exceptional fragrance throw.

Our founder, a master perfumer with over 20 years of experience, personally crafts each scent to evoke memories, emotions, and a sense of place.

"Where fragrance meets flame" isn't just our tagline - it's our philosophy. We combine the art of perfumery with the craftsmanship of candle making to create products that engage all the senses.
